La Carte 17

Logline

In Conakry, a former journalist receives the clandestine call of a young Congolese woman trapped in a Gulf city. When his first move puts her in danger, he sets out to find her and discovers that exposing the truth can also condemn the women he wants to save.

Short synopsis

La Carte 17 follows a man haunted by intimate loss, drawn back toward investigation and responsibility. By answering a young woman looking for a way out abroad, he enters a moral race where every published detail, every contact and every silence can reshape the fate of women kept invisible.

The project is a dramatic thriller rooted between Conakry and a fictional Gulf city, with investigative tension driven by guilt, memory and the price of public speech.
