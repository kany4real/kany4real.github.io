Une Clé à Conakry

Feature film · Warm urban family dramedy · In development

He wanted a room of his own. He will have to learn what a key really opens.

Feature filmFamily · Freedom · Conakry

Logline

In Conakry, a young man from a Congolese family pretends to be engaged so he can rent a room of his own, only to discover that freedom costs more than rent.

Short synopsis

Henry, 19, wants to be treated as an adult. To secure a room in a city where housing requires income, guarantees and reputation, he invents an engagement. The lie opens a door, then draws his family, a young woman and an entire neighbourhood into its consequences.
