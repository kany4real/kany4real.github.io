Signature project

Sortie de Route

Supernatural thriller series · African tech-noir · 52 min · In advanced development

In a hyperconnected city, some reflections do not return your image. They call to you.

Logline

In Yaoundé, a young ride-hailing driver and a disgraced inspector investigate a series of impossible deaths linked to reflections and screens, as an invisible presence tightens its grip around them.

Pitch

Yaoundé moves fast: ride-hailing apps, smartphones, cameras, screens everywhere. Ajara N’Dri drives her car to finance a future she imagines elsewhere. Kouakou, a disgraced inspector, still carries the marks of an old case that left him isolated from his hierarchy. When a series of impossible deaths appears to connect the victims to reflections, dark screens and ordinary surfaces turned disturbing, their paths collide.

What they face is neither a conventional killer nor a simple superstition. Sortie de Route builds an urban supernatural thriller where police investigation meets human drama, and where contemporary technology becomes the mirror of an older threat hidden inside what the city refuses to see.

Narrative Promise

A tense investigation in a connected city, driven by a fractured duo and by a threat that turns everyday tools into zones of fear.

World

The series grounds the fantastic in contemporary urban Africa: roads, screens, ride-hailing work, buildings, family memories and buried beliefs coexist without decorative folklore.

Themes

Technology and memory, buried memory, urban invisibility, intimate debt, survival, rational and irrational fear.

Public Characters

  • Ajara N’Dri: a young ride-hailing driver determined to regain control over her life.
  • Kouakou: a disgraced inspector marked by an old case and by what he believes he recognizes.

Stage

World, series bible and presentation materials developed.
