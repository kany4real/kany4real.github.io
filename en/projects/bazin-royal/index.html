Bazin Royal

Logline

In Yaounde, the inheritance of a bazin dynasty awakens a family debt that wealth never erased. In the Soga family, every succession becomes a struggle for the name, power and memory.

Short synopsis

Bazin Royal unfolds as a contemporary family saga around a Cameroonian textile empire. Between blood loyalties, economic ambition and intimate fractures, the series looks at how a fortune can become a debt passed from one generation to the next.

The project is conceived as a popular dramatic series universe focused on inheritance, rivalry, family memory and the conquest of a power that leaves no one untouched.
