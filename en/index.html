Writer · Director · Screenwriter · Series Creator

Kany J. Henry is a Congolese writer-director, screenwriter and series creator based in Conakry. He develops genre-driven films and series rooted in African realities, shaped by characters under pressure, moral dilemmas and a strong visual approach from the page onward.
